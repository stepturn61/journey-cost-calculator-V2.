How to Use This Calculator

Select your fuel type and the price displayed is the national average from over 7,000 filling stations across the UK, updated at the date displayed at the top. You can input your own price too for a more accurate price

Ways to Lower Your Fuel Consumption

Saving fuel isn't just good for your wallet; it's good for the environment. Here are proven ways to get more miles out of every gallon:

1. Maintain Steady Speed ▼
Rapid acceleration and hard braking are the biggest wastes of fuel. Use cruise control on highways to maintain a constant speed and improve your MPG by up to 14%.
2. Check Tire Pressure ▼
Under-inflated tires create more rolling resistance, forcing your engine to work harder. Check your tires monthly; proper inflation can improve gas mileage by up to 3%.
3. Reduce Excess Weight ▼
An extra 100 pounds in your trunk can reduce your fuel economy by about 1%. Clear out the golf clubs, heavy tools, and unnecessary cargo.
4. Avoid Excessive Idling ▼
Idling gets 0 miles per gallon. If you're going to be parked for more than 60 seconds (and it's safe to do so), turn off the engine.
5. Use AC Wisely ▼
At lower speeds, rolling down the windows is more efficient. At highway speeds, open windows create aerodynamic drag, making the AC the more fuel-efficient option.
